Welcome to Basket Hoop

About the Game

In Basket Hoop, gravity is your only challenge, and tapping is your sole weapon. Your goal? Simple: make the ball jump and send it inside the hoop!

Make as many baskets as you can in a limited amount of time. Collect coins with every basket you make to upgrade your ball and court!

How to Play

  1. Tap or click anywhere on the screen to make the ball jump.
  2. You can jump mid-air to control the angle and land the ball in the hoop.
  3. You have 5 seconds to score. If you miss, the ball resets for another attempt.
  4. Successful shots create a new net in a different position.
  5. Keep scoring before time runs out to beat your high score!
