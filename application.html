Welcome to Basket Hoop

About the Game

Basket Hoop is a fast-paced tapping game where gravity is your only challenge. Make as many baskets as possible before time runs out!

How to Play

  1. Tap or click to make the ball jump.
  2. Control the angle mid-air for better shots.
  3. You have 5 seconds to score each shot.
  4. Each basket changes the net's position.
  5. Keep scoring to beat your high score!
